How It Works


Have you ever been standing in your kitchen hungry and faced with a fridge full of tasty ingredients that you didn't know what the heck to do with? So have we!

 

A chef would walk into that same kitchen and whip up something delicious in minutes. I'm not a chef, you say, but let us help you feel like one.

 

Simply click on the 10 or fewer ingredients that you have in your kitchen with which you could cook tonight. Our app will return three curated recipes and you simply choose the one you would like for dinner. No need to run to the store, just get to cooking and eating.

 

Give the recipes you like a “thumbs up” and the ones you don't like a “thumbs down.” The more often you vote on the recipes, the better we will be able to give you ones you will love, based on your preferences – think Pandora for cooking. For every “thumbs down” we will give you a new recipe so you will always end up with something you want to make.

 

Finally, we assume that you have the basics in your kitchen – such as olive oil, salt, pepper, butter, lemon juice, sugar, Mlour, broth, canned tomatoes. Check here for the full list of items we assume you have in your pantry.
